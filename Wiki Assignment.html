Arjun Reddy
Arjun Reddy
Arjun Reddy is a 2017 Telugu film written and directed by debutant Sandeep Reddy Vanga and produced by his brother Pranay Reddy Vanga on Bhadrakaali Pictures banner. In this film, Arjun Reddy, is unconventional, free-spirited and tries to break the shackles of traditional societal norms. The film stars Vijay Deverakonda and Shalini Pandey in lead roles alongside Rahul Ramakrishna, Kamal Kamaraju and Kanchana portraying supporting roles. The soundtrack was composed by Radhan and background score is done by Harshavardan "Ramee" Rameshwar. Raju Thota served as the film's cinematographer while Shashank was the film's editor. The film tells the story of a young and brilliant medico titular character, Arjun Reddy with anger management issues.The film deals with the contemporary issues in urban youth upon love failure and received critical acclaim and gained a cult following among youth in two Telugu states. After working in Kedi (2010) and Malli Malli Idi Rani Roju (2015) Sandeep Reddy Vanga conceived the story of Arjun Reddy failed to find a producer due to the bold content. The film was released on 25 August 2017 with positive reviews and lead performances have been praised and threw the director into limelight for dealing the movie exceptionally. The film has faced controversies and censor issues but has received extremely positive reviews. The film is being remade in Tamil Nadu as Varma by Bala (director) who has won a National award as best director in 2009.

Plot

Arjun Reddy's (Vijay Deverakonda) breakup pushes him to the verge of breaking down. The story begins with Arjun practicing medicine in Hyderabad. Despite being high-functioning doctor he is in a lot of pain and using various coping mechanisms, such as abusing drugs, attempting one night stands and alcohol to deal with the pain. A flashback reveals his past. Arjun is a final year student/house surgeon at a medical school in Mangalore. Despite being a brilliant student, he gets into trouble because of his inability to control his anger. He is also very aggressive which doesn't help his cause. He had a great group of friends, including Shiva, who constantly looks out for him. 4 years ago Arjun falls in love with a fresher/first year student, Preethi Shetty. He goes around campus asserting that this girl is now exclusive to him.He starts showering Preethi with his idea of love and affection, going so far as to even set her up with a fat friend, because he is of the opinion that "a fat chick and a cute chick is an awesome combination". Eventually Preethi reciprocates and they fall head long into an intense physical relationship. Two months later, she moves in with Arjun, to the house he and his friends have rented. They have a beautiful relationship till Arjun gets his MBBS degree. Then, he clears post-medical exams to get admitted for MS. Orthopaedics in Mussorie. He moves to Mussorie and the duo often fly to meet each other. Over a course of 3 years their relationship becomes stronger, and Preethi enters Final year as House surgeon. Arjun and Preethi don't meet for six months when Arjun is taking his Final exams. Then, he goes to Preethi's house to invite her over to function. They cuddle together and make out on her balcony, when her father walks in. Enraged, he throws Arjun out of the house. Preethi's father opposes their relationship as they belong to different castes. Arjun delivers an ultimatum to Preethi that if she doesn't make a decision within 6 hours, their relationship is over. Following this incident, Preethi's phone is seized by her parents as to have no way to contact Arjun. When she finally comes to his house, he doesn't open the door to her as he's self injected himself with drugs and has gone into a semi-coma for two days, during which she's hastily married by her father to someone from their caste. Upon finding out, Arjun is devastated and creates chaos in both his and Preethi's house, and is kicked out by both. With his talent, he gets immediately employed and rents an apartment. He then goes on a downward spiral and subsequently becomes an alcoholic and a drug addict. He continues to perform surgeries even though he is intoxicated and has a 100% success rate, with 245 surgeries in 200 days. 7 months after the wedding, despite Shiva's attempts, he's unable to forget Preethi. He convinces one of his young patients, a leading film star to have a no-strings relationship, but rejects that when she falls in love with him. One day, when he is on leave and high on drugs, the hospital head nurse begs him to attend to an emergency. Unwillingly he agress, but collapses during the surgery due to dehydration. Though he regains consciousness and under his supervision, the nurses save the patient, the Hospital chief files a medical criminal case on him. Though Arjun's brother makes favorable case for him, he accepts blame during the hearing, which leads to him being banned from practicing any medicine for five years. He's also kicked out of his apartment the same day. He spends the night sleeping on the road with his dog, named Preethi. Shiva finds him the next day at a cafe and informs him that his grandmother has died. This sparks a change in him, and he goes back to his home, takes responsibility and consoles everyone, including his dad, and reconciles with his family. After being suggested by his father to take a vacation abroad, he agrees. On the way to the airport, he sees Preethi in a park, and she is pregnant. He's heartbroken, and decides on his trip to talk to her once he gets back. He meets Preethi, and clears all misunderstandings with her. He also finds out that she's fled her home three days after her wedding, and didn't even allow her husband to touch her, and tells Arjun that the baby is his. They both are delighted and kiss and make up. They both get married and Preethi's father realizes his mistake, not knowing how pure their love was. The movie ends on a happy note.

Production

After working in films such as Kedi (2010) and Malli Malli Idi Rani Roju (2015), Sandeep started writing the script for Arjun Reddy. Sharwanand was offered the lead role. But due to his date problems he casted Vijay Deverakonda as the lead for his directorial debut.[5] No producers were coming forward to do this bold film, he then his brother and father have started a company "Bhadrakali Pictures" and produce themselves.[6] Sandeep claimed that the film was partially derived from his college days.[7] Shalini Pandey, theatre artist from Jabalpur was selected as lead actress and she also dubbed in her own voice on director's insistence.[8]

Controversies

Anchor Anasuya Bharadwaj stated the film was encouraging and popularizing a cuss word that is disrespectful to women, especially mothers.[10] Congress Rajya Sabha MP V. Hanumantha Rao stopped a TSRTC bus and tore down the posters of Arjun Reddy.[11] A filmmaker, D Nagaraju from Khammam issued notices to the makers of Arjun Reddy and claimed that they have plagiarized his script.[12] Various women's groups protested against 'Arjun Reddy' in Vijayawada and demand ban on film's screening citing that huge posters of lip locks on city buses and public places are disturbing.[13]

Box-Office

Arjun Reddy completed 50 days of its theatrical run in October 2017.[14] The film grossed ₹ 51 crore at the worldwide box office.[3] The movie has went on to be a trendsetter and gained a cult status.

Critical reception

Arjun Reddy received good reviews. Neeshita Nyayapati of Times of India gave the film 4 stars, praising the dialogues, characters, instances in the film.[15] Sangeetha Devi Dundoo of The Hindu called it a landmark film.[16] Suresh Kavirayani of Deccan chronicle gave the film 4 stars, call it a sensation.[17] Vishnupriya Bhandaram of Firstpost, however lashed out the film for its nod to toxic masculinity.[18] Actor Mahesh Babu praised the film saying its a "path breaking film" and the direction is "top-notch". Director S. S. Rajamouli, Telangana IT Minister KT Rama Rao, and actress Samantha Ruth Prabhu praised Arjun Reddy for the novel concept of the film.[19][20]
